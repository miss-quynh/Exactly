"If we don't change, we don't grow. If we don't grow, we aren't really living."

Achieving self-improvement one experiment at a time.

Posted on January 20, 2019

This past year has been a year full of experiments for me as I was aching for a new career, new hobbies, and new experiences. I can confidently say that I have learned and grown so much as an individual, both personally and professionally. Every significant instance from this past year has shaped and prepared me as I welcome my quarter.

Speaking of welcoming my quarter, I had the wonderful opportunity to celebrate it with awesome people. Also, I was able to finally see snow! So obviously I had to partake in snow activities such as snowboarding, sledding, and playing snowball fight. I hurt every part of my body, but I would not trade this experience for anything. I felt super loved and spoiled.

Moreover, a quarter celebration is not complete until one has committed a quarter life crisis purchase. And I did just that ... I purchased my first car: 2019 Subaru Crosstrek Limited!
