I like how SHEIN has a Size Guide, so it definitely helped me in determining which size will fit me best, depending on the outfit. Be sure to utilize the Size Guide â€“ it is super helpful!

I went a little ham on my first SHEIN haul and ended up spending over $99, and I qualified for free express shipping! WOOT WOOT! Everything came in less than a week. Since I was super satisfied with my purchase, I decided to get another haul from SHEIN. I will post another blog on that at a later time.
